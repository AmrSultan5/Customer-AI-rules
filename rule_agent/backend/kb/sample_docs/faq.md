# Acme Widgets FAQ

## What sizes are supported?

Widgets come in three sizes: small, medium, and large. Medium is the default.

## How do I change a widget's color?

Set the `color` field in the widget's section of `acme.toml`, or pass
`--color <name>` to `acme new`. Supported colors are blue, green, red, and black.

## Where are logs stored?

Local runs write logs to `./.acme/logs/`. You can change this with the
`log_dir` setting in `acme.toml`.

## Is there a hosted version?

Yes. Acme Widgets Cloud runs widgets on managed infrastructure. Sign up at
the Acme dashboard and connect your project with `acme login`.

## How do I get support?

Email support@example.com or open an issue in the Acme Widgets repository.
Support requests are answered within two business days.
